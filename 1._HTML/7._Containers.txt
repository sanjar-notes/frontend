====== 7. Containers ======
Created Tuesday 01 September 2020

* There are two "container" tags in HTML.
* These tags are used to group together other elements/containers.

1. <**div**></**div**> - block level container, automatic newline.
2. <**span**></**span**> - inline level container(stacks horizontally)

* According to the box model, both div and span change width w.r.t the content.
